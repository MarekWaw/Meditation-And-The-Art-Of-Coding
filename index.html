Google, Facebook and other top tech firm employees are using meditation. Course such as Neural Self Hacking are popping up within their companies. These companies have made contemplative practices key features of their new enterprises, holding regular in-office meditation sessions and arranging for work routines that maximize mindfulness. 5

Here are the steps:

Take a deep breath.

Sit up straight on a folded blanket or comfortable pillow.

Close your eyes, and rest your hands on your thighs.

Check in with how you’re feeling.

Consciously bring your focus to your breath.

Start doing a body scan. Notice yourself in the moment.

Don’t try to manipulate the breath or do it any special way other than breathe into the abdomen.

Try and empty the mind. Notice thoughts come and go. If one starts to take hold, release it without self-judgment. Let go of all you can mentally and physically.

Notice how this natural rhythm happens by itself.

Slowly open your eyes and notice how you feel.
